About Me

Discover the passion and stories behind my art journey.

Hello!

I am Helena, a passionate artist living in Alanya. My journey in the visual arts began in the 2000s. Over the past 20 years, I have been constantly improving my skills, learning various techniques and trying myself in many areas of my craft.

Through my work, I strive to inspire emotions, tell stories and connect with those who are interested in my work around the world.

My Mission

To create art that resonates with people, evokes emotions, and brings beauty to everyday life. I strive to make a meaningful impact through my creativity.

Milestones

  • 2022: Collaborated with international galleries
  • 2025: Launched the "Inspire" collection
