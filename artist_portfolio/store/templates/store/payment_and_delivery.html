Payment and Delivery

Payment Methods

We offer several payment methods for your convenience:

  • Bank Transfer: Pay directly to our bank account. Details will be provided during checkout.
  • PayPal: Secure payment via PayPal. Choose this option during checkout to complete your payment.
  • Credit/Debit Card: Pay via credit or debit card using our secure payment gateway.
  • Cryptocurrency: We accept payments in selected cryptocurrencies. Contact us for further details.

Note: All payments are processed before shipment. Orders will only be shipped after full payment is received.

Delivery Information

Orders are processed and shipped only after the payment is complete. We offer shipping to various locations worldwide.

Free Shipping: For orders over $1000, we provide free delivery.

Delivery Times: Standard delivery times are between 5-10 business days depending on your location. Please note that delivery times may vary due to the pandemic or other circumstances beyond our control.

If you have any questions regarding payment or delivery, feel free to contact us for more information.
